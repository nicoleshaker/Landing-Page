Make Me A Playlist

This is a webpage I designed and developed that takes four user inputs to generate a unique, personalized Spotify playlist. I used HTML, external CSS, and JavaScript. I created it in 2021 for a school project with the intention of helping my classmates discover new music. The four user inputs required to produce the playlist are “Artist You Like,” “Song You Like," “Genre You Like,” and “Genre You Want to Get Into.” After the user inputs all the values, they click the "MAKE ME A PLAYLIST" button, after which a unique token is generated. I use this token to produce the link to the playlist, which the user can then listen to and add to their library.

News AI Chatbot

This is an AI chatbot that I and another software engineer deisgned and developed. The idea is that the chatbot gives users news about topics that they are interested in. You tell it what you want to know about, and it searches the latest news articles for the most relevant pieces, finally providing you a short summary/headline and a link to the article, so that you can read further if you choose.
